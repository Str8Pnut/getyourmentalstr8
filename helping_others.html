Being a Supportive Friend

Supporting someone with mental health challenges starts with listening without judgment, showing empathy, and encouraging professional help when needed.

What You Can Do

  • Be present and listen actively
  • Learn about mental health conditions
  • Offer help with daily tasks or appointments
  • Respect their journey and boundaries
  • Encourage professional support
