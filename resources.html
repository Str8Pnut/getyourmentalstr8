Emergency Hotlines

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Emergency: 911

Digital Tools

Explore mental health apps, meditation guides, and support forums to stay connected and informed. Here are some helpful digital tools:

  • Headspace – Guided meditation and mindfulness resources
  • Calm – Meditation, sleep, and relaxation app
  • 7 Cups – Free emotional support and online therapy
  • Sanvello – Stress, anxiety, and depression support app
  • Moodpath – Mental health screening and journaling
